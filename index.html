About Bhuvanaranga Travels

Since 2013, Bhuvanaranga Travels has redefined road travel in Whitefield and greater Bengaluru. From seamless airport transfers to Kempegowda International Airport to unforgettable outstation adventures—whether you’re off to the historic palaces of Mysore, the lush coffee plantations of Coorg, or the misty hills of Munnar—our expert team has you covered. Choose from self-drive hatchbacks, luxurious chauffeur-driven sedans, or rugged SUVs for city explorations, and enjoy our spacious tempo traveller and mini-bus options for group outings, corporate retreats or weekend getaways. With a meticulously maintained fleet, real-time flight monitoring, crystal-clear pricing and friendly, professional chauffeurs, we guarantee punctuality, comfort and peace of mind. Experience the pinnacle of Bengaluru taxi service, outstation cabs and tempo traveller rentals—all backed by over a decade of unwavering commitment to your journey.
